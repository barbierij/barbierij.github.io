Joseph Barbieri work about

docurama films


Docurama Films is a distributor of full-length and short-form documentaries and non-fiction TV programming.

As subject matter varied wildly per release, there was a need to be flexible with styles and to adapt strategies to the specifics of each release. The challenge was to represent the films themselves but to also engage the unfamiliar viewer.

For Bruce Weber, I wanted to highlight the familiar black and white portrait style, then compliment with a romantic type approach.

With no available materials for Robert Drew: Ten Masterworks of American Cinéma Vérité, I created a bold collection logo to be used as the dominant element in the foreground and the background—offset by a sequence of smaller screenshots.

The challenge of The Paradise Lost Trilogy was updating an established campaign to which the filmmakers were quite attached while speaking directly to it in sequence. Refining the typography and adding palette saturation to the initial releases—incorporating stark portraits overlayed with inverted crosses of the lives involved—visually showcases the case of The West Memphis Three.
